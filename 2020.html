Hola, I build cool stuff at Rappi!

As part of the RappiPay team my mission is to improve the payment experience of both people and businesses across Latin America.

I built the bank statement microservice which lets our customers download their PDF statements from the app and get it monthly in their inboxes.

I also work on the system that lets our allies accept payments with QR codes. I am building internal tooling to make it easier to know what version of a service was deployed to an environment or country and when.
