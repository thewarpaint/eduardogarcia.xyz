How is "Mexico" pronounced in Spanish?
"Me-hee-co", you can listen to the pronunciation on Wikimedia.
Is Mexico in South America?
No. It's part of North America, together with Canada and the US. However, apparently some definitions of Central America comprise Mexico.
Are "hard-shell tacos" Mexican food?
No. Tacos have soft tortilla made of corn flour, sometimes of wheat flour.

There are, however, a few dishes in Mexican cuisine that use hard tortillas, for example: tostadas, a flat, fried or baked, corn tortilla; and tacos dorados, literally "fried tacos".

No, Taco Bell not a Mexican restaurant, it serves Tex-Mex style foods.
What language is spoken in Mexico?
The great mayority of the population speaks Spanish, but also dozens of indigenous languages are spoken, such as Nahuatl and Maya.
Can a Mexican person understand Spanish from Spain, and the other way around?
Yes. As with any other language, there are regional variations such as pronunciation, different words and expressions, etc. but they're not an obstacle for communication.
