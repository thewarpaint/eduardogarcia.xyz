How is "Mexico" pronounced?
"Me-hee-co"
Is Mexico in South America?
No, it's part of North America. Depending on who you ask to, it might or might not be included in Central America. But not for Mexican people.
Are hard-shell "tacos" Mexican food?
No. Tacos usually have soft tortilla made of corn flour. And Taco Bell is a Tex-Mex restaurant.
What language is spoken in Mexico?
Mostly Spanish, but also dozens of indigenous languages, such as Mayan and Nahuatl.
Can a Mexican person understand Spanish from Spain, and the other way around?
Yes. As with any other language, there are regional variations, words and expressions, etc. that you would need help with. But it's not an obstacle for communication at all.
